This website is all about Gugas Buns


Gugas Buns is the respective name for the famously-attained hamburger Bun
created and developed by Gustavo “Guga” Tosta, a chef and internet steak enthusiast.

First released in 2019, "Gugas Buns", the unoffical official name for Guga's
hamburger bun recipe, has since been popularized in subsequent videos on Gugas
YouTube Channel (Guga Foods) and other miscellanous cooking and recipie sites online.
